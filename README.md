# How to build it and play it yourself?
1. Build the circuit (the schematics and images of the circuit in the TinkerCad are in this repository)
2. Connect the circuit to the PC and start the Arduino System
3. Open the Max/MSP and turn on both the audio outputs [ezdac~] and toggles (one at the beginning of the serial communication with Arduino and another connected, at the BPM Counter, to the [metro 200] object
4. Open and play the Processing Sketch
